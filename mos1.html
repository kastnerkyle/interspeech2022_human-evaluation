LISTENING TEST

Welcome, and thank you for participating in this listening test.



INSTRUCTIONS:

Try to rank sounds based on the delivery rate, and style rather than on audio quality.

For example, something that sounds like a baseball announcer but with static (e.g. interference over a car radio) should be considered in the 4 or 5 (natural delivery) categories. If the audio quality is so bad it interferes with your listening, it can be ok to score lower however.

A clearly voice with unnatural delivery but perfect, crystal clear audio quality should be considered more in the 3, 2, or 1 categories - unless the timing and style feels "natural" to you (then it can be scored higher).


Rate each example as independently as possible, some of the sentences may be duplicates but try to rate separately without doing direct "head-to-head" comparison.

There will be a total of 21 questions - scroll down to see the audio player, and begin.

Thank you again for participating!






Rating system for this test, and approximate description of the score for each value

  • 5 (Natural delivery)
  • 4 (Somewhat natural)
  • 3 (Acceptable/Neutral)
  • 2 (Somewhat unnatural)
  • 1 (Very unnatural)
  • 0 (Not applicable / did not answer)
